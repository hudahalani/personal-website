Project Manager - Accessibility Engineering Project

University of Toronto Engineering Curriculum

Project Overview

Served as project manager for a team of five engineers developing innovative solutions to accessibility challenges within a UofT building. Led comprehensive problem analysis, solution development, and project delivery using AutoCAD for technical design.

Problem Statement

Addressed accessibility challenges within a University of Toronto building, identifying barriers and developing user-centered solutions that met accessibility guidelines and regulations while ensuring practical implementation.

Leadership Role

  • Project Planning: Developed timelines and milestones for team of five engineers
  • Team Coordination: Facilitated communication and collaboration among team members
  • Resource Management: Allocated tasks and resources efficiently
  • Stakeholder Communication: Regular updates to faculty advisors and clients

Technical Implementation

  • AutoCAD Design: Created detailed technical drawings and floor plans
  • 3D Modeling: Developed comprehensive 3D models of solutions
  • Accessibility Solutions: Mobility, visual, and hearing accessibility improvements
  • Cost Analysis: Conducted thorough cost-benefit analysis

Project Outcomes

Successfully delivered innovative accessibility solutions that exceeded client expectations. Demonstrated effective project management and technical leadership in addressing real-world engineering challenges.
