Team Leader - Engineering Design Project

Engineering Strategies and Practices II

Project Overview

Led a team of seven engineers in developing a solution to environmental pollution caused by steel-corroding parking blocks. Utilized Fusion 360 for design and collaborated closely with client to deliver an innovative, environmentally-friendly solution.

Problem Statement

Traditional steel parking blocks were corroding over time, releasing harmful pollutants into the environment. Our team designed an alternative solution that eliminated corrosion while maintaining functionality and cost-effectiveness.

Leadership Role

  • Project Coordination: Ensured guidelines were followed and deadlines met
  • Team Management: Coordinated seven engineers with clear role distribution
  • Client Relations: Regular communication and progress updates
  • Quality Assurance: Maintained high standards for all deliverables

Technical Implementation

  • Fusion 360 Design: Created detailed 3D models and technical drawings
  • Material Selection: Researched and selected corrosion-resistant materials
  • Prototype Development: Designed and tested multiple iterations
  • Performance Analysis: Validated design effectiveness through testing

Project Outcomes

Successfully delivered innovative solution that impressed the client with extensive research and attention to detail. The project demonstrated effective team leadership and technical problem-solving capabilities.
